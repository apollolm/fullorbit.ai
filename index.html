Frequently Asked Questions

Is this just hype, or do these tools really work?

I was skeptical too. But I personally built a feature in 8 hours that would have previously taken our engineering team 3-4 weeks. This isn't theory—it's my lived experience at leading technology companies. The tools are real, and the results are measurable.

Will AI replace our developers?

No. AI makes developers superhuman, not obsolete. Your team will build features faster, with better quality, while focusing on higher-level architecture and problem-solving. It amplifies human capability rather than replacing it.

What if our code is confidential or highly regulated?

Security is paramount. Both Claude Code and Google's Vertex AI offer enterprise-grade security controls. Claude Code uses read-only permissions by default and restricts access to specific project folders. Google Cloud offers zero data retention options and VPC Service Controls. For highly sensitive environments, we can implement additional safeguards including air-gapped deployments, client-side encryption, or on-premises solutions. Both platforms maintain SOC 2 Type 2 and enterprise compliance certifications.

How quickly will we see results?

Most teams see initial improvements within the first week of implementation. Significant productivity gains typically occur within 2-3 weeks as the team becomes comfortable with the new workflows and tools.

What if our team is resistant to AI tools?

I understand this concern - I was initially skeptical myself. In my experience, the best way to address resistance is through demonstration rather than persuasion. When people see these tools in action and experience the dramatic time savings firsthand, skepticism typically turns to interest. I focus on showing real examples and letting the results speak for themselves.

Do you offer leadership coaching alongside the technical training?

Yes. I conduct leadership team interviews and facilitate conversations that result in 1-3 actionable next steps to address organizational challenges around AI adoption. This ensures your transformation succeeds at both the technical and leadership levels, helping executives navigate the strategic implications of dramatically faster development cycles.

Do you offer a guarantee?

I'm confident in these tools because I've experienced the transformation personally. However, every team and situation is different. That's why I always start with a no-risk discovery call to assess whether this approach is a good fit for your specific situation. If we determine it's not the right fit, we simply don't move forward.
