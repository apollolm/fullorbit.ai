Frequently Asked Questions

The honest answers to questions every engineering leader asks about AI adoption.

What is agentic development?

Agentic development is when AI tools like Claude Code and Gemini CLI act as intelligent development partners—not just code generators, but collaborators who understand context, make strategic decisions, and handle complex workflows end-to-end. These tools can read your entire codebase, understand your architecture, write comprehensive tests, debug issues, and even help with planning and documentation. Unlike simple AI autocomplete, they work at the task level: "implement user authentication with OAuth" becomes a complete, tested feature rather than just a few lines of code. The result? Development cycles that previously took weeks now happen in hours, with better quality and test coverage than manual development.

How will AI help my dev team?

Developers using Claude Code can deliver features in hours that used to take weeks. They're solving harder problems, building better software, and becoming incredibly productive. AI tools handle the heavy lifting—reading codebases, writing tests, debugging issues, even planning architecture. Your team focuses on the creative problem-solving and strategic decisions. The teams mastering these tools first are gaining an enormous competitive advantage.

What about security and compliance?

Claude Code does send code snippets to Anthropic's servers for processing, but you control what gets shared through file selection and context management. For enterprises concerned about data exposure, Google and Anthropic have zero data retention policies, private cloud deployments, or on-premises solutions. Both also have SOC 2 and compliance certifications, and we'll work with your team to implement appropriate safeguards for your specific requirements.

What if my engineers don't trust AI-generated code?

Good. They shouldn't trust it blindly. AI is a powerful tool and thought-partner, not a replacement for thinking. But let's be honest about what Claude Code can actually do—it's like having a Senior Engineer, Analyst, QE, and technical writer all wrapped into one. The engineers I work with use it daily for key functionality development, troubleshooting, debugging, performance optimizations, planning and documentation. You still review everything, you still make the important decisions, but now you have an incredibly capable collaborator who can handle complex work across the entire development lifecycle.

What happens if we don't adopt AI development tools?

We're at an inflection point. Teams that master agentic AI development will ship faster, solve harder problems, and take on challenges that would have been impossible before. Meanwhile, teams still doing everything manually will find themselves explaining why projects take months instead of weeks. The gap is widening fast—if you're one step behind today because you haven't started with agentic AI coding, you'll be five steps behind tomorrow. The longer you wait, the harder it becomes to catch up. It's not about replacing good engineering—it's about amplifying what your best people can accomplish.

How long does it take to see results?

The productivity jump happens fast. My team improved within their first week using Claude Code. But getting everyone comfortable and optimized? Different story. Some developers adapt quickly, others need coaching through their skepticism. Full adoption, where AI-assisted development becomes your normal workflow, took us 6-8 weeks.The key is we don't just dump training on your team and leave. We guide the adoption process, help overcome resistance, and ensure the new workflows actually stick. That's why we include the executive coaching and follow-up assessment—sustainable change takes both technical skills and organizational support.
